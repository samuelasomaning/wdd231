Gadget Pulse

Site Name

Gadget Pulse - This name reflects the blog's focus on the latest gadgets, innovations, and tech trends. It helps readers stay current with cutting-edge technology and insights.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Gadget Pulse is to provide detailed reviews, comparisons, and news about the latest technology and gadgets, helping tech enthusiasts stay informed and make smart decisions.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest smart device releases and their key features?
  • How do different laptops compare for gaming vs. professional use?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0077cc – Used for headings, buttons, and call-to-action elements.

Secondary Color: #f2e9fa – Used for background and content areas to maintain a clean, light feel.

Typography

Headings: "Oswald", Sans-Serif – Used for all headings to convey a bold and tech-focused feel.

Body Text: "Open Sans", Sans-Serif – Used for paragraphs and general text to ensure readability and modern style.
